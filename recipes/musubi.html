Musubi Recipe

Ingredients

  • Spam
  • Rice
  • Nori
  • Low Sodium Soy Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Furikake

Steps

  1. Cook rice
  2. Combine sugar and soy sauce
  3. Slice spam into 1/2 lengthwise
  4. Pan fry spam pieces for 5 minutes
  5. Glaze sauce over spam as it cooks
  6. Combine all ingredients
