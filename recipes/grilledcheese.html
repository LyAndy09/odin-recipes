Grilled Cheese Recipe

Ingredients

  • Sliced bread
  • RKraft singles cheese
  • butter

Steps

  1. Grab two slices of bread
  2. Spread butter on both slices
  3. Preheat pan on medium heat
  4. Place 2 slices of kraft singles onto one slice of bread, then cover with the 2nd piece.
  5. Add some butter to pan and place sandwich onto it.
  6. Allow cheese to melt before taking off pan.
